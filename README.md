# Task 1: Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Future Interns - Data Science & Analytics

**CIN ID:** FIT/MAY26/DS17705

---

##  Dataset

* Superstore Sales Dataset (9,995 Orders)
* Total Revenue: ₹22,94,505
* Total Profit: ₹2,85,703

---

##  Analysis Performed

### Top Products Analysis

Analysis of best-selling products based on sales and profit contribution.

### Regional Performance

Comparison of sales performance across different regions.

### Category Performance

Evaluation of sales performance across different product categories.

### Time Trends Analysis

Analysis of sales patterns over time including monthly and yearly trends.

---

##  Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards (Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders)
* Top Products by Sales
* Sales Trend Over Time
* Sales Distribution by Category
* Regional Sales Performance

---

##  Key Insights

1. Technology category generated the highest revenue.
2. West region was the best performing region.
3. Holiday season (December/January) showed peak sales trends.
4. Top-performing products contributed a major share of total revenue.
